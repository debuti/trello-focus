Trello focus is a way to visualize your most important Trello cards, no matter the board they are placed.

Steps
  • Go to trello and add the tag FOCUS to every card you want to see in the dashboard.
  • Grant access for this app to your data clicking here
  • Profit your custom dashboard.
  • Btw, icons are made by Smashicons from www.flaticon.com
  • Oh yes, and the source code is available at github.com/debuti/trello-focus
